The Little Prince Site Plan

Site Purpose

Over the years, the little prince has attracted people of all ages who have been amazed by the story of the little prince and his teachings. It has become a classic for adults, but it is being somewhat forgotten by the new generations. The purpose of this site is to arouse the interest of the new generations in this classic.

Target Audience

Kids and teens from 7 to 16 years old
